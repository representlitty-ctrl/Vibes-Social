Vibes is a social media platform designed specifically for vibecoders seeking a focused and supportive learning environment. It’s a place where like-minded individuals can connect without distractions or misaligned incentives. Vibes aims to make learning and building feel natural, social and motivating.

The platform encourages vibecoders to share their projects openly, whether they’re experiments, works in progress or finished products. By sharing builds and ideas users learn from each other in real time fostering faster growth. Knowledge sharing is central to Vibes with a focus on practical insights curiosity and continuous improvement.

Vibes also provides direct pathways to opportunity. Vibecoders can discover and apply for grants gain visibility for their work and access resources that support building and shipping. The platform’s goal isn’t just conversation but tangible outcomes that help creators progress.

While Vibes retains the enjoyable aspects of social media like expression interaction and community it’s shaped around a shared mindset.  Built by vibecoders for vibecoders every feature is thoughtfully designed with respect for the craft.

This repository showcases the active development of the Vibes platform. It reflects an evolving product driven by experimentation feedback and a long-term vision of a healthier and more purposeful social space for builders.

Vibes is still growing but its mission remains clear: learn vibecoding share knowledge build together and unlock real opportunities with like-minded individuals.
